# QPlanck divisions

The documentation is organized by division so that each part of QPlanck can
develop a clear identity without losing its connection to the wider mission.

| Division | Role | Current documentation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| QCore | Open-source quantum circuit development, debugging, simulation, and interoperability | Architecture, research, RFCs, security, and roadmap |
| QPlanck Labs | Practical quantum learning and research environment | Initial architecture and phased delivery direction |
| TPH (The Planck Horizon) | Quantum intelligence newsletter | Editorial purpose, audience, and issue structure |

## Adding future divisions

When QPlanck expands, each new division should begin with:

1. a concise purpose and audience;
2. a boundary explaining what the division does and does not do;
3. a current-status statement;
4. links to its products, research, or publications; and
5. a roadmap that distinguishes committed work from exploration.

The top-level QPlanck pages remain the shared map. Detailed technical or
editorial content belongs within the relevant division.
