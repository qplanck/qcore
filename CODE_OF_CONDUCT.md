# Code of Conduct

QCore follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance,
race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Positive behavior includes:

- showing empathy and kindness
- respecting differing opinions and experiences
- giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, intimidation, discriminatory
language, sustained disruption, or publishing private information without
permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of
acceptable behavior. Reports may be sent to the security/contact address listed
in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) until a dedicated governance contact is published.
